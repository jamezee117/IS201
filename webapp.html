DOODLE JUMP CLONE

How to Play

• Press SPACE to start or restart.

• Move left with A or ←.

• Move right with D or →.

• The player auto-jumps after starting.

• Land on platforms to keep climbing.

• Springs (green coils) launch you higher.

• Avoid triangular obstacles (red).

• If a platform has a spring or obstacle, a safe partner platform appears.

• Score increases every 5 seconds.
